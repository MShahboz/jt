Jettonify

Welcome to Jettonify - a Meme Token Voting Mini App designed for the TON Hackers League Hackathon.

Home

Jettonify is a Telegram Mini App created to enable TON blockchain users to engage with meme tokens in a meaningful way. The platform provides a structured, fair, and transparent system for voting on meme tokens. By offering a progressive voting cost structure, the app encourages genuine support over artificial vote inflation, making it a valuable tool for both token developers and the community.

Tokens List

This section displays a list of available meme tokens that users can vote for. Each token includes its name, a short description, and the current number of votes it has received. Users can browse through the list to find tokens they want to support. By selecting a token, they can view more details and proceed to the voting section, where they cast their votes using TON.

Vote

In the Vote section, users can actively participate in supporting their chosen meme tokens by connecting their TON wallets and paying with TON. The voting cost is designed to increase progressively: the first vote costs $1 worth of TON, the second $5, and subsequent votes increase further. This rising price structure ensures that each vote is meaningful and limits token owners from artificially inflating their own token's votes.

Rewards Distribution

After each voting season, the total TON collected is distributed among the top-ranking tokens based on the following structure:

  • 1st Place: 30% of the total TON (50% of which is used to buy the token, and the remaining 50% is added to its liquidity pool with TON)
  • 2nd Place: 20% of the total TON
  • 3rd Place: 15% of the total TON
  • 4th Place: 10% of the total TON
  • 5th Place: 5% of the total TON
  • App Development and Promotion: The remaining 20% of the collected TON is used for maintaining the app, covering server costs, and funding promotional activities.

This rewards structure promotes token development by offering liquidity incentives and directly involves TON supporters in helping their chosen tokens grow.

Referral System

Jettonify includes a referral system to further engage users and expand the platform’s reach. Users can invite friends to participate in voting, and when a referred friend votes, the referrer earns 5% of the TON spent on that vote. Referral rewards accumulate in the user’s account and can be withdrawn once they reach a minimum balance of 1 TON or 5 TON. This system rewards active users and encourages platform expansion.

User Profile

The User Profile section allows each user to keep track of their voting history, current referral rewards, and total balance. This feature enables users to see their influence within the app, monitor their earnings, and manage their referral bonuses. It also enhances transparency and helps users stay engaged by providing a personal record of their interactions within the platform.

Token Development and Ecosystem Growth

Jettonify not only encourages token development by supporting liquidity for winning tokens but also fosters a collaborative TON ecosystem. By directing part of the voting fees into liquidity pools for the top tokens, the platform ensures ongoing support and stability for promising projects. The progressive voting cost structure prevents token owners from artificially boosting their tokens, allowing the community to genuinely support the tokens they believe in. The referral system adds an extra layer of engagement, helping Jettonify attract a wider audience and build a robust user base that supports the growth and stability of TON and its meme tokens.
